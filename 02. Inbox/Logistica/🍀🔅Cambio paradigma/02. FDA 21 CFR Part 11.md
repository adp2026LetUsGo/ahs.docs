It applies to FDA-regulated industries (pharma, biotech, medical devices) using computer systems for,,compliance data, requiring validation, audit trails, secure,restricted access, and documentation management. 

**Key Aspects of 21 CFR Part 11 Compliance**

- **Scope:** Applies to records created, modified, maintained, archived, or transmitted electronically under FDA predicate rules.
- **System Validation:** Systems must be validated to ensure accuracy, reliability, and consistent performance.
- **Audit Trails:** Computer-generated, time-stamped audit trails must be implemented to independently record the date and time of operator actions, such as creating, modifying, or deleting records.
- **Electronic Signatures:** Must be unique to an individual, password-protected, and linked to their respective electronic record to prevent falsification.
- **Security Controls:** Systems must limit access to authorized personnel, ensuring data integrity.
- **Documentation:** Written standard operating procedures (SOPs) for system use and training records are mandatory.
