## 2. Do You Need a License?

In the world of software, "license" usually refers to two different things:

- **Business License:** Generally, you do **not** need a specific U.S. license to sell software to Americans from abroad. However, you must comply with **Export Control Laws**. As long as your software isn't related to high-level military encryption or restricted dual-use technology, a simple "self-classification" is usually enough.
    
- **Software Licensing:** You need a digital license agreement (EULA) that tells the user they are "licensing" the software, not "owning" your code. This protects your intellectual property.
