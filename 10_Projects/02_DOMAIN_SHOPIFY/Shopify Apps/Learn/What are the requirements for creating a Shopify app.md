[Creating a Shopify app requires meeting several technical, security, and user experience requirements](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[2](https://www.shopify.com/partners/blog/how-to-build-a-shopify-app)[3](https://www.designrush.com/agency/shopify/trends/shopify-app-development)[4](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create):

**Technical Requirements**:

- [You need to have a Shopify Partner account and a development store](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create)[4](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create).
- [You should have Node.js 18 or higher installed](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create)[4](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create).
- [You should have a Node.js package manager: either npm, Yarn 1.x, or pnpm](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create)[4](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create).
- [You should have Git 2.28.0 or higher installed](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create)[4](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create).
- [You should be using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[4](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/getting-started/create).
- [Knowledge of any backend programming language, including Node.JS, Java, PHP, Ruby on Rails, and Python](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[3](https://www.designrush.com/agency/shopify/trends/shopify-app-development).

**Security Requirements**:

- [Your app must not require a desktop app to function](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).
- [Apps that falsify data to deceive merchants or buyers are not permitted](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).
- [Apps that process payments outside of Shopify’s checkout are prohibited](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).

**User Experience Requirements**:

- [Apps that are pieces of standalone software that need to be downloaded to a computer are not permitted](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).
- [Apps that mostly rely on merchant or Partner interaction to operate should consider listing as a service in Shopify’s Partner Directory](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).
- [Apps that make little or no use of Shopify’s APIs are not permitted](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).

[Please note that these requirements are subject to change, as Shopify continuously makes improvements to the Shopify App Store and developer platform](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements). [Your app will be expected to meet any new requirements that are added](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements). [The Shopify App Review team can reject an app at their discretion if it doesn’t meet the set standards](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements)[1](https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/store/requirements).
